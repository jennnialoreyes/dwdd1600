Advice That Inspires My Design Journey

"Design is about solving problems, not decorating them."
This advice reminds me to always think about the purpose behind every choice I make from color to typography.
"Create from what you feel, not just what you see."
These words helped me connect more deeply with my projects and express emotions through visuals, not just shapes.
"Progress is better than perfection."
This thought keeps me motivated when I doubt my skills. It reminds me that every project is a step toward becoming a better designer.
