Who Were the Signers

Most famous signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Indepedence include

Thomas Jefferson
Main author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
Strong advocate for independence, later the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, diplomat, and statesman who helped secure French support for the American Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large and bold signature.
Samuel Adams
Leader of the Sons of Liberty and a key figure in organizing resistance to British rule.
